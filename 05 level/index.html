John Keating

John Keating is a beloved English teacher portrayed by Robin Williams in the 1989 film "Dead Poets Society." He teaches at the prestigious Welton Academy, an all-boys preparatory school in Vermont.

Keating is known for his unconventional teaching methods and his passion for literature and poetry. He inspires his students to think for themselves, seize the day (carpe diem), and pursue their dreams, rather than conforming to societal expectations.

Throughout the film, Keating challenges his students to break free from conformity and embrace their individuality and creativity. He encourages them to appreciate the beauty of poetry and literature as a means of expressing their emotions and exploring the complexities of life.

John Keating's character embodies the transformative power of education and the importance of inspiring young minds to question the status quo and pursue their passions.
